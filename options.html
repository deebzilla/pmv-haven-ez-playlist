PMV Haven Playlist Helper

Paste the playlist URL you want to use, or just the playlist ID. After that you can:

Right-click a PMV Haven video link and add it directly, or click the extension icon to add every open PMV Haven video tab in one shot.

Tip: the helper uses the PMV Haven login already present in this browser. If add requests fail, open PMV Haven and make sure you are signed in there.

Video URLs are matched from forms like /video/some-title_123....
